Frequently Asked Questions

What should my portfolio show?

Include range of scales - streetscapes, districts, public spaces. Show design process and final products.

What software is expected?

Adobe Creative Suite, SketchUp, Rhino, AutoCAD, and GIS are common. Strong visualization skills are essential.

How do I show implementation experience?

Discuss projects that were built or approved. Show you understand real-world constraints and construction.

What about interdisciplinary work?

Urban design involves many disciplines. Discuss collaboration with engineers, planners, and other professionals.

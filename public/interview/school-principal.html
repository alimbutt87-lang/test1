Frequently Asked Questions

What leadership experience do principal candidates need?

Most positions require teaching experience plus administrative credentials. Highlight experience leading teams, managing budgets, and implementing school-wide initiatives.

How do I demonstrate instructional leadership?

Discuss how you've improved teaching practices, used data for decisions, led professional development, and supported struggling teachers.

What questions should I expect about discipline?

Be prepared to discuss your philosophy on student behavior, restorative practices, and balancing accountability with support.

How do I address school turnaround experience?

Share specific examples of improvements you've led with measurable outcomes in student achievement, culture, or operations.

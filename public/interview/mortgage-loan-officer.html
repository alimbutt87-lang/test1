Frequently Asked Questions

What licensing is required?

NMLS registration and state licensing are required. Ensure your licenses are current and discuss your testing and continuing education.

How do I demonstrate production ability?

Be prepared to discuss your volume history, conversion rates, and strategies for generating business. Quantify your track record where possible.

What loan products should I know?

Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, jumbo, and specialty products are common. Highlight breadth of experience and complex scenarios you've handled.

How do I show compliance awareness?

Discuss your understanding of TRID, fair lending, and disclosure requirements. Show you take compliance seriously while still closing loans.

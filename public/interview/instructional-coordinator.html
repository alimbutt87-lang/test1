Frequently Asked Questions

What teaching experience is expected?

Strong classroom teaching experience is typically required, often 5+ years. Demonstrate how your teaching experience informs your approach to supporting other educators.

How do I show leadership and coaching abilities?

Give examples of mentoring teachers, leading professional development, facilitating teams, and guiding instructional improvement. Highlight specific outcomes from your leadership.

What data skills do employers expect?

Experience analyzing student achievement data, identifying trends, and using data to inform instructional decisions is essential. Discuss specific tools and processes you've used.

How do I address teacher resistance?

Be prepared to discuss building trust, involving teachers in decision-making, providing support during transitions, and demonstrating respect for professional expertise while promoting growth.
